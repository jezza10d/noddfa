Our History: The Heart of the Valley

Located in the center of Glyncorrwg, Noddfa has been a vital hub for our village for generations. The name ‘Noddfa’ translates to ‘Sanctuary’ in Welsh, a fitting title for a place that has provided a home for local culture, connection, and community spirit since the industrial era.

For many years, the building was well known and loved as the village Senior Citizens Centre. However, when the facility faced the threat of closure, the people of Glyncorrwg refused to let this essential space disappear.

A dedicated group of local volunteers rallied together, united by a shared vision to keep the doors open for everyone. Through their hard work and determination, they took over the premises, ensuring the building remained in the hands of the community.

Today, Noddfa stands as a proud testament to that volunteer spirit. It has evolved into a modern multi-purpose center where you can hire rooms for celebrations, attend local events, or simply find a friendly face. By preserving our past, we are making sure there is a vibrant "Sanctuary" for Glyncorrwg’s future.
