About the Event

The Artisan Summer Fair features a curated selection of handmade jewelry, pottery, paintings, and textiles. Enjoy live music throughout the day from the Glyncorrwg Community Choir and local acoustic performers.

Feeling hungry? Our refreshments area will be stocked with treats provided by local bakeries, and we'll have a range of hot food stalls including artisan wood-fired pizzas and local Welsh specialties.

All proceeds from the raffle and refreshment stalls support the center's youth programs and ongoing maintenance of our community facilities. Come along, support local businesses, and enjoy a wonderful day out with the whole family!
